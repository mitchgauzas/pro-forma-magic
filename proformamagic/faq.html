General

Pro Forma Magic is a real estate analysis platform built for anyone involved in evaluating or presenting property — buyers, sellers, brokers, property managers, appraisers, analysts, mortgage brokers, and underwriters. If you work with real estate in any capacity, it gives you the tools to analyze any property and present your findings professionally.

Yes. Pro Forma Magic is available worldwide. Pricing is displayed in your local currency and reflects purchasing power parity, so it's fairly priced relative to your local market.

Who It's For

Buying a property is one of the largest financial decisions you'll ever make. Pro Forma Magic gives you a clear, complete picture of the numbers before you commit — cash flow projections, mortgage scenarios, cost breakdowns, and return estimates — so you can evaluate any property with confidence, negotiate from a position of knowledge, and avoid surprises after the deal closes.

You can also build a long-term investment forecast that factors in planned renovation costs and capital improvements over time — so you understand not just what a property is worth today, but what it becomes after your work is done. Once you own it, track actual performance in real time against your original forecast, so you always know whether the investment is delivering on plan and can make informed decisions when it isn't.

A well-priced listing backed by data sells faster and at better terms. Pro Forma Magic lets you build a polished financial analysis that clearly demonstrates your property's income potential and investment value — giving serious buyers a compelling, credible reason to move forward at your asking price. It turns a good listing into a persuasive one.

Your clients expect expertise at every step. Pro Forma Magic lets you deliver a professional-grade financial analysis for every property you present — branded with your information, exportable as a polished PDF, and ready to share or present on screen during meetings and video calls. When every touchpoint looks this sharp, you don't just close deals — you build a reputation that generates the next one.

Managing multiple properties means constantly tracking performance across a portfolio. Pro Forma Magic gives you a structured financial picture of each asset — income, expenses, cash flow, and returns — making it easy to spot underperformers, benchmark against market expectations, and present clear, professional results to property owners. It turns scattered numbers into decisions you can act on.

Speed and consistency matter when you're managing a volume of assignments. Pro Forma Magic's structured templates let you organize income, expense, and valuation data efficiently — cutting the time spent on formatting and layout so you can focus on the analysis itself. Every output is clean, professional, and consistent, which reflects well on your practice and the reports you deliver.

Whether you're screening a single asset or working through a pipeline of opportunities, Pro Forma Magic gives you a consistent, professional framework for every analysis. The platform is flexible enough to produce a quick summary or a detailed deep-dive — so you can move fast on initial reviews and go deep when the deal warrants it. Standardized outputs also make it easy to compare properties and present findings clearly to stakeholders.

Helping your clients understand the full picture before they sign is what sets you apart. Pro Forma Magic lets you run mortgage scenarios, stress test cash flows at different rate assumptions, and show borrowers exactly how the numbers work on any property — building their confidence and yours. When clients feel informed and well-guided, deals close faster and referrals follow.

As an underwriter working on behalf of a lender, your job is to confirm that the numbers behind a deal hold up — and that the income being projected is realistic enough to support the debt being requested. Pro Forma Magic gives you the structured, consistent data you need to do that efficiently: clear cash flow statements, debt service coverage ratios, and detailed income and expense breakdowns that make it straightforward to assess whether a property can carry the loan.

Consistent, well-organized reports reduce the back-and-forth with borrowers, flag weak assumptions early, and give your institution the documentation it needs to make a confident lending decision — and defend it.

Development projects carry some of the most complex financial inputs in real estate — and the margin for error is slim. Pro Forma Magic lets you build a complete project feasibility model from the ground up: start with land acquisition costs, layer in your hard construction costs (site work, materials, and labour), and add soft costs (permits, architecture, engineering, legal, and financing charges) to arrive at a true picture of total project cost before a shovel goes in the ground.

Whether you're developing a single building, a multi-phase condominium, or a subdivision of lots, you can model each component independently or as part of the whole — tracking cost per unit, cost per square foot, and overall project returns across the full development timeline. Run your numbers through to disposition — selling individual units, lots, or the completed asset — and understand your profit margin, return on cost, and break-even point so you can make confident go or no-go decisions and present a credible case to lenders and investors at every stage.
